Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Middle-earth and Your Privacy

Middle-earth is a standalone lore and reference app focused on characters, realms, events, journeys, and lineages from the world of Middle-earth.

Privacy is a simple principle for this app: your data stays on your device.

At this time, Middle-earth does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to developer-controlled servers.

What We Do Not Collect

Middle-earth does not collect or process:

  • your name
  • your email address
  • your phone number
  • your location
  • your contacts
  • payment information
  • advertising identifiers
  • analytics or behavioral tracking data

The app also does not use:

  • third-party analytics SDKs
  • advertising SDKs
  • cookies
  • profiling tools
  • user account systems

Local Data on Your Device

To make the app usable and personalized, some data may be stored locally on your device only.

This may include:

  • favorites
  • collections you create
  • language preferences
  • certain app settings and interface preferences

This local data is not sent to us and is not stored on external servers controlled by the developer.

Offline-First Behavior

Middle-earth is designed primarily as an on-device reference experience. Core content and user preferences are intended to remain available locally within the app.

If external links or source references are opened by the user, those destinations operate under their own privacy practices.

Third-Party Services

Middle-earth does not currently use third-party services for:

  • analytics
  • advertising
  • user tracking
  • account management

If this changes in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or alongside that change.

Children’s Privacy

Middle-earth does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any other users.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised from time to time if the app’s features or data practices change. If that happens, the updated version will be published here with a revised date.
