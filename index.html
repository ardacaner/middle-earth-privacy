Privacy Policy

Atlas of Arda and Your Privacy

Atlas of Arda is a standalone reference app focused on characters, realms, events, concepts, journeys, and lineages from the world of Middle-earth.

This app is designed to work as a local, reader-focused experience. At this time, Atlas of Arda does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to developer-controlled servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

Atlas of Arda does not collect or process:

  • your name
  • your email address
  • your phone number
  • your location
  • your contacts
  • payment information
  • advertising identifiers
  • analytics or behavioral tracking data

The app also does not use:

  • third-party analytics SDKs
  • advertising SDKs
  • account systems
  • cookies
  • profiling or tracking tools

Data Stored on Your Device

To support app functionality, some information may be stored locally on your device only. This may include:

  • favorites
  • collections you create
  • language preferences
  • certain appearance and interface settings

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to developer-controlled servers.

External Links and Sources

Atlas of Arda may include references to external sources or links selected by the user. If you open an external site, that site operates under its own privacy practices and policies. Atlas of Arda is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

Third-Party Services

Atlas of Arda does not currently use third-party services for:

  • analytics
  • advertising
  • user tracking
  • account management

If this changes in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Children’s Privacy

Atlas of Arda does not knowingly collect personal data from children or from any other users.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time if the app’s features or data practices change. If that happens, the updated version will be published with a revised date.
